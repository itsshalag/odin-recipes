Chicken Wrap

Description:

Made with healthy ingredients and range free chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Tomatoe
  • Lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Ranch

Steps

  1. Place chicken on skillet on high medium cook until done.
  2. Cut chicken into pieces and place on warm burrito tortilla.
  3. Cut up you tomatoes, avo and lettuce.
  4. Place all ingredients in the tortilla and add ranch.
